FAQ

What is WTDN.com?

WTDN stands for 'What to do next.' We are a career and educational resource for anyone tyring to figure out what they should do next so that they can achieve their dreams.

Is WTDN right for me?

If you are a college student looking to pick a major, an adult looking to make a career change, or a lifelong learner with a desire to explore the idea of learning something new, then WTDN is right for you!

How much do your courses cost?

Our courses are Free!

How long does a course take to complete?

We aim to make our courses no longer than 5-10 hours.

Will I learn everything I need to know about a subject by completing a course?

No. WDIDN is designed to help you get enough of a sense of a subject to determine whether you truly like it and to understand if a subject will fit your needs.

Questions? Concerns? We want to hear from you!
